Dongfeng Subdistrict (simplified Chinese: 东风街道; traditional Chinese: 東風街道; pinyin: Dōngfēng Jiēdào; literally: "east wind") is a subdistrict of Qiaodong District, in the heart of Shijiazhuang, Hebei, People's Republic of China.[1] As of 2011[update], it has 5 residential communities (居委会) under its administration.[2]
