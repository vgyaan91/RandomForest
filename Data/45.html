Paramarpissa is a spider genus of the Salticidae family (jumping spiders).[citation needed]

Species[edit]

  • Paramarpissa albopilosa (Banks, 1902) — USA, Mexico[full citation needed]
  • Paramarpissa griswoldi Logunov & Cutler, 1999 — USA, Mexico[full citation needed]
  • Paramarpissa laeta Logunov & Cutler, 1999 — Mexico
  • Paramarpissa piratica (Peckham & Peckham, 1888) — USA, Mexico
  • Paramarpissa sarta Logunov & Cutler, 1999 — Mexico
  • Paramarpissa tibialis F. O. P.-Cambridge, 1901 — Mexico
