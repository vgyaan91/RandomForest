Raddan (Persian: ردان‎, also Romanized as Raddān, Radān, and Rādān)[1] is a village in Keraj Rural District, in the Central District of Isfahan County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,597, in 425 families.[2]
