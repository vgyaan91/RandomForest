Thirukkoloor is in Thoothukudi district in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. It is next to Alwarthirunagari on the Tirunelveli - Tiruchendur Highway, Tamil Nadu, Southern India.

Sri Vaithamanidhi Perumal temple[edit]

This place is one of the 108 Divya desams, and one of the Nava Tirupatis.[clarification needed] The Lord is in kidantha thirukkolam. This is the place where Kubera[who?] got his wealth back.
